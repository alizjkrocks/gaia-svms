Prompt 01 — Coherence Check

Read your three files for alignment and contradictions

This prompt sends your three skill files to Claude for a full coherence analysis. Claude reads across all three simultaneously and returns structured findings — what aligns, what creates tension, and what contradicts.

How to use this prompt
01Copy the prompt below
02Open Claude.ai and start a new conversation
03Paste the prompt, then replace [PASTE WORLDVIEW.MD], [PASTE BRAND.MD], and [PASTE EXECUTION.MD] with the actual contents of your files
04Send and review the report Claude returns
05Update any files that need it, then move to Layer 5
Copy this prompt
You are running a coherence check on a three-layer skill system built by a solopreneur. Read across all three files simultaneously and identify where they align, where they create tension, where they contradict, and where something is implied but missing. Be direct and honest. This is a diagnostic, not a validation. Do not manufacture positives. If there are contradictions, name them. worldview.md is always the highest authority — if execution.md or brand.md contradicts it, name it clearly. --- WORLDVIEW.MD: [PASTE WORLDVIEW.MD CONTENT HERE] --- BRAND.MD: [PASTE BRAND.MD CONTENT HERE] --- EXECUTION.MD: [PASTE EXECUTION.MD CONTENT HERE] --- Produce a structured report with clearly labelled findings: - COHERENT: Where the layers align and reinforce each other. Name specifically what aligns and why it strengthens the system. - TENSION: Where two layers pull in slightly different directions. Name exactly which files and exactly what to resolve. - CONTRADICTION: Where something directly contradicts another layer. Name the exact conflict. worldview.md is always the authority. - GAP: Something implied in one layer but missing or underdeveloped in another. Name what's missing and why it matters. Include 4-6 findings. At least one of each type if they exist. Reference their actual words, not generic observations. End with a SYSTEM SUMMARY — a single paragraph in third person that distills the complete picture: who this person is, what they believe, who they serve, how they work, and what makes their approach distinctly theirs. Make it specific enough to use as a bio or an AI context-loader.
