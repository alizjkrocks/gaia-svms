Three skill files. Five layers. One system that makes any AI work from your actual philosophy, your actual voice, and your actual methods — not a generic starting point.

"Most people use AI as a faster typist. This turns it into a collaborator that thinks like you."

What you build
File 01
worldview.md
Your philosophy. What you believe, what you stand for, what you won't do. The root of the system — everything else answers to this.
File 02
brand.md
Your identity. How your beliefs show up commercially — your voice, your values in action, your position, your non-negotiables.
File 03
execution.md
Your methodology. The real process, the conditions that produce your best work, the principle that makes your output distinctly yours.
After Layer 3, you have a working system. Paste all three files at the top of any AI conversation and it works from your foundation — not a blank slate.

Layers 4 and 5 deepen it: coherence checking across the three files, and a routing OS (system.md) that tells your AI exactly which file to consult depending on the task.
The five layers
Layer 0120–30 min

→ worldview.md

Layer 0215–20 min
Brand Builder
→ brand.md

Layer 0320–25 min
Execution Builder
→ execution.md + System Summary
12 questions — your real process, conditions for excellent work, the constants that never change. Ends with a visual System Summary.
Layer 0410–15 min
Coherence Check
→ Scored diagnostic report
12 self-guided questions read across all three files. Surfaces alignment, tensions, contradictions, and gaps — with specific actions.
Layer 0515–20 min
Workflow Builder
→ system.md + file annotations
Maps how your AI moves between the three files depending on the task. Generates a routing OS and annotations to add to each file.
Ongoing2 min
Update Checker
→ Clear / Tension / Contradiction verdict
Run every time you want to add something to a file. Five questions, one verdict, one next step. Prevents contradictions before they compound.

How to use your files
01
In any AI conversation
At the start of a session, paste the contents of your skill files before giving your task. The AI reads your foundation first and works from it throughout the session.
02
In a Claude Project (free)
Create a Project in Claude.ai. Add your files to the Project Knowledge. Every conversation inside that project has your files automatically loaded — you never paste again.
03
As Claude Skills (Pro or higher)
Each file is already formatted as a Claude skill. To upload: create a folder named worldview, rename your file to SKILL.md, zip it, and upload via Settings → Capabilities → Skills. Repeat for each file. Claude loads them automatically in relevant conversations.
04
Keep them updated
As your thinking evolves, use the Update Checker before adding anything new. Prevents contradictions that compound silently over time.
